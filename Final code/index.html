How Integral is GDPR to Business

This site explores how the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) helps keep user information safe in online systems. It emphasises important rules mentioned in Article 5 and the requirements for consent as stated in Article 7. Visitors can participate in the material through an engaging quiz, while the platform showcases real-world GDPR application, such as managing consent, storing data, and user rights.
