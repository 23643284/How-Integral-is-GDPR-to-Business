# How-Integral-is-GDPR-to-Business
This application is a prototype of the GDPR informative quiz developed as a part of my third and final year university.

This project investigates the practical application of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) within a web-based platform, concentrating on Article 5 (Data Processing Principles) and Article 7 (User Consent). The goal is to show that GDPR can be integrated directly into the design of systems instead of being viewed solely as an abstract principle or policy requirement. This application serves as an engaging platform that both informs users and illustrates the real-life application of GDPR.

Objective

The objective of this project is to create and develop an interactive web application that illustrates GDPR compliance in action, enhances user awareness of data protection principles, and encourages a privacy-focused design approach in digital systems.

When a visitor accesses the website, they are initially greeted with a consent notification. After granting their consent, they can participate in the GDPR quiz, with their results being saved locally afterwards. The analytics are displayed on the dashboard according to their performance. Users also have the ability to download or email their results and can erase their data whenever they wish.
